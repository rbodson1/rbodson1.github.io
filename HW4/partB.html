Card Matching Game

    Instructions

  • There are three levels of difficulty easy, medium and hard.
  • You must choose a diffculty level in the beginning before you can play.
  • In easy mode, you have 3 seconds to view the images before they flip over. You have 16 tiles and 120 seconds to complete the game.
  • In medium mode, you have 5 seconds to view the images before they flip over. You have 20 tiles and 150 seconds to complete the game.
  • In hard mode, you have 8 seconds to view the images before they flip over you have 24 tiles and 180 seconds to complete the game.
  • Click Start Game to begin.
